Bajaj Super, Bajaj Priya / FE (R)

Item Code: AB-001

We offer a wide range of brake shoes for Bajaj Super, Bajaj Priya / FE (R). These brake shoes are induction hardened to prevent them from wear & tear. The brake shoes are durable and reliable owing to our stringent quality mechanisms. We make use of best material, method and at the same time comply with industry standards to make these replacement parts.  Our brake shoes for Bajaj Two Wheelers are compatible to the original braking system. These have linings that are either riveted or bonded for good strength and friction.
