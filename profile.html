Why Us?

The latest breakthrough by our team is asbestos free lining. It is a bitter truth, that asbestos which is used in many industrial products like brake shoes, disk pads, cement, roof shingles, textile insulations, cigarette filters, flooring processes, etc. is very dangerous to human health. Inhaling asbestos particles can cause serious lung disease and other abnormalities. Even a brief exposure to asbestos particles can cause abnormalities difficult to cure. Asbestos free lining in brake shoes offers superior softness, better grip and extra long life to the brake shoes.
 We, at Autocraft Brake Systems constantly endeavor to make our environment asbestos free.
